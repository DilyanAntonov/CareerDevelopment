Our AI-powered Fit Score feature helps you understand how well your resume matches the job listing you're interested in. Here's how it works:

1. Resume Analysis: Our system takes the information from your resume, including your summary, education, experience, skills, languages, projects, and interests.
2. Job Listing Analysis: We then analyze the details of the job listing, such as the job title, description, requirements, location, and employment type.
3. AI Evaluation: Using advanced AI algorithms, we compare your resume to the job listing and generate a fit score between 0 and 10, with 10 being the best match.
4. Results: You receive a short analysis explaining the fit and a score to help you understand how well your profile matches the job.

This feature helps you make informed decisions and target the job opportunities that best match your qualifications.
